About Us

Hope for All supports child education and women's empowerment through community programs, skill training, and micro-support. We build programs that help people learn, earn, and lead.

  • Scholarships & resources for children
  • Workshops & mentorship for women
  • Vocational & digital skills training

Our Work

Child Education

Books, tuition support and after-school programs for children.

Women Empowerment

Workshops, mentorship and small grants to empower women.

Skill Training

Hands-on digital & vocational training for employability.
